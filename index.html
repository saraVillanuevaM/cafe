Digital Experience for Cafès Roure

The Cafès Roure project has posed an exciting and significant challenge for our team from the outset. Our task was to translate the essence and charm of a humble yet inviting place like Roure into the digital realm. Our goal was to craft an online experience that would capture the essence of specialty coffee for a diverse audience, including those who may not consider themselves experts in the field. Understanding the complexity of this endeavor, we chose to place the user at the forefront, guiding our actions and decisions.

Throughout this project, we have ventured into various concepts and aesthetics, pushing ourselves beyond our comfort zones and embracing new ideas. Our aim was to develop a web platform that not only conveyed the unique character of Cafés Roure but also enabled the brand to reach new heights and connect with fresh audiences. We trust that this project will motivate and foster future approaches and innovations in the field of design and user experience.
